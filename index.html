General AI Assistants

ChatGPT

All-purpose assistant for writing, coding, research, and images.

AI search engine with cited sources for quick research.

Multi-step autonomous research reports, built into ChatGPT.

Google's autonomous web research agent, built into Gemini.

AI research assistant for searching and summarizing academic papers.

Search engine that pulls evidence-backed answers from research papers.

Coding

AI pair programmer integrated into popular IDEs and GitHub.

Open-source AI pair programmer that works in your terminal.

Open-source AI code assistant, customizable with any model.

Autonomous coding agent that runs inside VS Code.

Google's AI tool for generating and editing images and cinematic scenes.

High-quality, stylized AI image generation.

Open-source image model, highly customizable and self-hostable.

Microsoft's AI assistant with built-in image generation powered by DALL-E.

Google's AI filmmaking tool for generating and editing cinematic video scenes.
